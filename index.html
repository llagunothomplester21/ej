Learn Something New

Check out these fun facts and activities:

  • Did you know? The largest animal in the world is the blue whale!
  • Fun Activity: Draw your favorite animal and give it a name.
  • Did you know? Rainbows are made when sunlight passes through raindrops.
  • Did you know? A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance".
  • Fun Activity: Try to list 5 animals that can fly!
  • Did you know? Honey never spoils; archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient tombs.
  • Fun Activity: Imagine a new insect and design it with different colors and wings.
